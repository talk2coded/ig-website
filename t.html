About IGHub

InnovationGrowth Hub is the first of its kind in South east Nigeria, located in a state with the highest small and medium enterprise in the country.We work with start-ups at every stage of maturity to provide the tools, resources, knowledge and expertise they need to succeed. We are committed to empowering vibrant entrepreneurs by providing a platform for business incubation, tools, resources, training and mentorship needed to grow your idea into a big business. We are invested in building an ecosystem where local businesses can function optimally and a community of entrepreneurs where idea can be shared, problems analyzed and solutions proffered. This community includes individual developers, designers, business-experts, entrepreneurs, researchers, engineers, technologists looking to launch start-ups.
